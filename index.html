The Bogan-Martin Award

The Bogan-Martin Award is given yearly to the Flickr staff member
who inadvertently generates the most spectacular media overreaction
to a personal comment, inside joke or bug.

Previous Winners

  • 2017: Phil Dokas
  • 2011: Timoni West
  • 2010: Daniel Bogan
  • 2009: Chris Martin
  • 2008: Stewart Butterfield
